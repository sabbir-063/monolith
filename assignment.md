Problem Statement
The Mission: You have been hired to sell the most boring object on the planet: a plain red brick.

Your goal is to build a one-page online store that makes this brick look like a premium, must-have luxury item. But there is a catch: the modern internet user has an attention span of about 3 seconds. If your website is boring, they will leave.

The Challenge: Using AI, you must build a website that absolutely refuses to let the user get bored. Alongside a beautiful, premium design. You must include at least one or more of the following interactive elements:

1) A Mini-Game

2) Scrolling Transition

3) Interactive 3D or Animations

Judging Criteria (What We Are Looking For):

We are looking for the perfect balance between high-energy interactivity and effortless usability. Your site will be graded on

1. Fluidity: Do your animations, scrolling, and transitions run seamlessly without lagging?

2. Intuitive Design: Is the site immediately easy to navigate and understand?

3. Frictionless UX: Do your interactive elements (minigames, video, 3D) enhance the user experience without cluttering the screen or confusing the user?
